## Technical Setup Notes for Recording:

### Prerequisites:
- MetaMask installed and connected to Sepolia testnet
- Contract deployed to Sepolia
- Two wallets prepared: one patient, one doctor
- Frontend running on localhost:3000

### Recording Settings:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- Frame rate: 30 FPS
- Screen capture tool: OBS Studio or similar
- Audio: Clear voiceover with minimal background noise

### Post-Production:
- Add background music (low volume, healthcare/tech themed)
- Include subtle transition effects between scenes
- Ensure text overlays are readable
- Add captions for accessibility

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: Medical blue (#1E88E5)
- Secondary: Success green (#43A047)
- Accent: Lock gold (#FFC107)
- Background: Clean white (#FFFFFF)

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid:

- Don't show wallet private keys or seed phrases
- Avoid using real patient names or data
- Don't make medical claims or diagnoses
- Keep technical jargon minimal in narration
- Ensure all on-screen text is large enough to read
